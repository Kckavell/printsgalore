Five methods

Decoration
services

We don't push one method. Every process produces a different result — we'll tell you which one is right for your garment, your design, and your order size.

9-COLOUR

DTF
Direct-to-Film

A vivid, durable transfer printed in up to 9 colours on film, then heat-pressed onto your garment. DTF works on virtually any fabric type — cotton, polyester, blends, nylon — and produces a bright, flexible print that holds up wash after wash.

Best for: bold graphics, multi-colour designs, small to medium runs, and prints on dark garments.

9-COLOUR

DTG
Direct-to-Garment

Ink printed directly onto the garment using a specialist inkjet printer. DTG handles photographic detail, smooth gradients, and complex multi-colour artwork that other methods struggle with.

Best for: photo-quality prints, intricate designs, and one-off or short-run orders on 100% cotton.

Get a DTG quote →
PREMIUM

Embroidery

Thread stitched directly into the fabric using a CNC embroidery machine. The result is a raised, textured decoration that adds real weight and quality to a garment — you can feel the craft.

Best for: logos, branding marks, chest badges, and caps. Outlasts any printed method and signals premium quality at a glance.

Get an embroidery quote →
ALL-OVER

Sublimation

Colour dye transferred into the polyester fibres of the fabric under heat, becoming part of the garment itself. This means no cracking, no peeling, and a vivid all-over finish that covers the garment edge to edge.

Best for: sports kits, performance wear, and all-over graphic garments on white or light polyester fabrics.
