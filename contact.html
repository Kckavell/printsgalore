Common questions

Do you have a minimum order quantity?

No fixed minimum. One hoodie or one thousand — we quote on everything. Larger runs get better unit prices, and we'll always tell you the break points.

What file format do you need for printing?

Vector files (AI, EPS, PDF) give the sharpest results. High-resolution PNG (300dpi+) also works well. Not sure? Send what you have and we'll let you know if it's print-ready.

How long does a typical order take?

Turnaround depends on the method and quantity. We'll give you a realistic timeline with your quote — and we stick to it.

Can I see a proof before you print?

Yes — we send a digital proof for approval before production starts. Use our design preview tool to get an early feel, then we refine it from there.
