Understanding GSM

What GSM means for you

GSM stands for grams per square metre — it's the weight of the fabric. It directly determines how the garment feels in your hands and on your body.

A low GSM (140–180g) is light, breathable, and drapes loosely — ideal for summer events, sports, and everyday tees. A high GSM (300–380g) is substantial, structured, and warm — the kind of hoodie you keep for years.

Not sure which weight suits your brief? Tell us the end use and we'll recommend the right blank.

GSM weight scale

140–180 GSM LIGHTWEIGHT

Breathable, soft drape. Summer tees, race events, giveaways.

180–240 GSM MID-WEIGHT

Versatile everyday weight. The go-to for branded team tees.

260–320 GSM HEAVY

Structured, warm, quality feel. Sweatshirts and mid hoodies.

320–380 GSM PREMIUM

Heavyweight premium hoodies. Substantial drape, long lifespan.
